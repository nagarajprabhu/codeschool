CodeCoach Description


CodeCoach is an Indian online interactive platform that offers free coding classes in different programming languages including Python (pandas-Python library, Beautiful Soup-Python Library), Java, Go, JavaScript (jQuery, AngularJS, React.js), Ruby (Ruby on Rails-Ruby framework), SQL, C++, Swift, Sass etc. As well as markup languages HTML and CSS. The site also offers a paid "Pro" option that gives users access to personalized learning plans, quizzes, and realistic projects.


platform aimed at professional adults and students. we have made a special effort to attract corporate trainers seeking to create coursework for employees of their company. As of 2020, there are more than 1lakh courses on the website. Students take courses largely as a means of improving job-related skills.

History


CodeCoach was founded in August 2017 by Nagaraj, Anish and Mahesh. Nagaraj graduaed from Bangalore University to focus on launching a venture, and Anish and Mahesh who also graduated from the same university in 2017 started this online course. In February 2018, the founders tried to raise venture capital funding, but the idea failed to impress investors and they were rejected 30 times.


Within a few months, 1,000 instructors had created about 2,000 courses, and we had nearly 10,000 registered users. Based on this favorable market reaction, they decided to attempt another round of financing, and raised Rs. 10lakh in venture funding by August 2018.

Partners

In September 2019, CodeCoach partnered with Amazon for free Alexa skills training.

It had also raised Rs. 50lakh from groups such as Union Square Ventures and Naspers.

In February 2020, we also raised Rs. 50lakh from long-time partners in Maharastra, Tamilnadu and announced Rs. 1cr valuation.
